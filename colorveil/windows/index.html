ColorVeil. A customizable color filter over your screen. Reduce eye strain, help with Dyslexia / Irlen.

A coloured tint across the screen helped students with their reading (Middlewich High School)

Free Download for Windows

Color Filter

ColorVeil adds a completely customizable color filter over your Desktop, Internet browser, video player, document, email, or program. You can work, play, read, write, or browse as usual.

Dyslexia / Irlen

ColorVeil can help people with Dyslexia / Meares Irlen's Syndrome. It applies a coloured tint across the screen, and allows full customisation to match the colours stated by the optometrist.

Extra Dimmer

ColorVeil can reduce screen brightness, even below its lowest available setting. This feature helps you reduce eye strain, especially at night, and may help save battery on notebooks and tablets.

Key Features

Customizable

ColorVeil lets you easily customize the filter color and opacity. Choose Orange to block out the blue light responsible for sleep deprivation, or any other color for any other reason.

Easy On/Off

ColorVeil makes it easy to turn the color filter on and off: simply left-click its icon in the notification area, at the far right of the taskbar. Or press a (customizable) keyboard shortcut.

Stealth & More

ColorVeil can go stealth and hide its turn off switch, helping you play pranks on your friends or family. It can also spice up screencasts or presentations, by adding a color filter to the video output.
