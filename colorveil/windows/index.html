Screen Color Filter

ColorVeil adds a completely customizable color filter over your Desktop, Internet browser, video player, document, email, or program. You can continue to work, play, read, write, or browse as usual.

Dyslexia / Irlen

ColorVeil can help people with Dyslexia / Meares Irlen's Syndrome. It applies a coloured tint across the screen, and allows full customisation to match the colours stated by the optometrist.

Dim Screen Brightness

ColorVeil can reduce screen brightness, even below its lowest available setting. This feature helps you reduce eye strain, especially at night, and may help save battery on notebooks and tablets.
