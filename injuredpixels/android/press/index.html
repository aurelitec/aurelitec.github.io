InjuredPixels for Android Press Kit

Short Description

Easily check your PC, laptop or tablet screen for dead pixels, scratches or defects. Use the portable version to test screens in the store, before buying, or during the warranty period, as you may get a get a replacement according to the manufacturer's dead pixel policy. InjuredPixels fills the entire screen with a primary or custom color, helping you easily spot pixels that do not match the selected color. Tiny, free and open source.

System Requirements

InjuredPixels runs on all Android versions starting with version 4.4.
